Call for projects
RADR Lab DeFi Project Incubator is designed to invest in and support third-party DeFi startups, motivate global developers, reward ecosystem constructors and support ecosystem constructions. Also, it invests in quality projects and participates in project development as the early governance party.
The lab can not only provide financial support for quality projects that participate in RADR DeFi project incubation, but also provide all-round support and incentives such as offering liquidity incentives, assisting in improving ecosystem liquidity and token lock-up, helping warm up and accelerate quality projects.
Just need to send an E-mail to dev@radarlab.org for incubation application (mail title starts with ‘DEFI Incubator’) with information such as project name, website, introduction, contact, type, valuation and expected fundraising. The lab will screen projects based on products, ecosystem value, team, outlook, among other things, and provide ecosystem support for the final winner.
